Features

Freeze Apps

Disable apps without uninstalling. Keep your data intact while stopping unwanted apps.

Battery Control

Restrict background activity and manage wake locks to extend battery life.

Batch Operations

Apply actions to multiple apps at once with progress tracking.

Activity Launcher

Launch hidden activities from any app. Access secret settings and features.

Action Logs

Track all operations with timestamps. Rollback battery actions when needed.

System Protection

SafetyValidator blocks critical system apps from being disabled.
